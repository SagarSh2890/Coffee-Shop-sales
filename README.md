# Coffee-Shop-Sales-Dashboard-Analysis
Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard designed to analyze revenue, footfall, product performance, and order patterns.
The dashboard helps in understanding customer behavior, peak hours, store performance, and product contribution.

🎯 Business Objective

Track overall sales and customer footfall

Identify peak ordering hours

Analyze category-wise and size-wise sales distribution

Compare store-level performance

Identify top-performing products


📊 Key KPIs

Total Sales: $698,812.33

Total Footfall: 149,116

Average Bill per Person: 4.69

Average Orders per Person: 1.44

⏰ Hourly Sales Trend

Peak order quantity observed between 8 AM – 10 AM

Sales decline after afternoon hours

Morning hours are the highest revenue contributors


📦 Category Distribution

Coffee category contributes the highest share (~39%)

Tea and Bakery also show strong performance

Some categories contribute very minimal share


📏 Size Distribution

Regular size orders dominate (~39%)

Large and Not Defined also contribute significantly

Small size has lowest contribution

![image](https://github.com/SagarSh2890/Coffee-Shop-sales/blob/main/Screenshot%20(99).png)



📅 Weekday Orders

Higher order volume observed mid-week

Slight drop towards weekends



💡 Business Recommendations

Increase staffing during peak morning hours

Promote high-performing products via combo offers

Optimize low-performing categories

Focus marketing on Regular and Large size orders

Improve weekend promotional strategies
